Our Story

Founded in 1996, Hiraeth Guitars has been building the finest hand-crafted instruments in Wales for over 20 years. Meticulous and impeccable build quality; we only use the finest timbers from around the world. All tonewoods used by Hiraeth Guitars are sustainably sourced from ethical wood mills.
